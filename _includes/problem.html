Pharma's $200 billion paradox

Why AI Predictions Fail in Humans

The AI revolution was meant to fix pharma's 90% failure rate. Instead, it's hitting the same wall: a fundamental lack of predictive, systemic data. This is the 'Systemic Data Gap'.

Siloed Biology

Cellular assays, pre-clinical models, and human clinical data exist in separate worlds.

Scalability Desert

Current high-throughput methods (in vitro) are scalable but simplistic, with less than 40% translatability.

Reductionist Models

All today's available methods miss the multi-organ, systemic effects that cause drugs to fail in humans.

Bridging these translational gaps is the critical barrier to predictive drug discovery.
